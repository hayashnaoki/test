# Sushi Rolls

## Build Steps

1. **Prepare rice**
   Cook rice. Season with sushi vinegar. Cool to room temperature.

2. **Prepare workspace**
   Wrap bamboo mat with plastic. Cut fillings into long, thin strips. Prepare tezu.

3. **Assemble**
   Place nori (shiny side down) on mat. Spread 1/2–3/4 cup rice evenly, leaving ~1 inch uncovered at the far edge.

4. **Add fillings**
   Arrange fillings in a line across the center. Do not overfill.

5. **Roll**
   Roll tightly using the mat, applying even pressure.

6. **Seal & shape**
   Moisten nori edge with water, finish roll, gently shape into a cylinder.

7. **Cut**
   Slice into 6–8 pieces with a wet, clean knife.

8. **Serve**
   Serve immediately with condiments.
